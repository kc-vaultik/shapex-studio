## Notes for Designer

- **Tone**: Confident, aspirational, action-oriented (like Stripe/Linear)
- **Avoid**: "Revolutionary", "game-changer", "disrupting"
- **Use**: "Validated", "actionable", "proven", "fast"
- **CTAs**: High contrast, clear hierarchy (primary vs secondary)
- **Social proof**: Place testimonials near pricing for credibility
- **Mobile-first**: Hero headline must work on small screens (break into 2 lines if needed)
